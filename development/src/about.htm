About

I am a generalist. Those in the software engineering industry might describe me as "full stack", but I think it's more than that. Part interaction designer, part software engineer, I have gone to great lengths to learn as much as I can over the years.

I've spent the majority of my career writing code, but have led and contributed to UX and visual design projects at every opportunity. I have found that being able to think in both of dimensions simultaneously results in clear, efficient solutions, with a minimum of overhead both in implementation and infrastructure.
